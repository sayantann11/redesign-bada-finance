Loan products

Six product journeys, each written to be understood quickly.

Instead of repeating generic text, this services page gives each loan type a sharper use case so the customer can self-select before they enquire.

H

Home Loan

For purchase, resale, balance transfer, and top-up needs with longer tenure flexibility.

  • Competitive repayment structure
  • Ideal for salaried and self-employed buyers
  • Useful for first homes and upgrades
P

Personal Loan

For fast unsecured funding when the need is urgent and collateral is not preferred.

  • Medical, travel, education, renovation
  • Shorter processing path
  • Clear monthly affordability planning
B

Business Loan

For expansion, inventory, working cash, hiring, or capacity creation in growing businesses.

  • Growth capital support
  • MSME use cases
  • Operating cash-flow alignment
L

Loan Against Property

For larger borrowing needs backed by residential or commercial real estate value.

  • Higher ticket potential
  • Longer tenure than many unsecured products
  • Suitable for business or personal goals
W

Working Capital Loan

For operational continuity when receivables, inventory, or seasonality tighten cash flow.

  • Inventory and payroll continuity
  • Short-cycle funding support
  • Built for day-to-day business rhythm
C

Car-Backed Loan

For borrowers who want liquidity while keeping their existing vehicle in active use.

  • Asset-backed approach
  • Fast conversation-driven screening
  • Useful for urgent working needs
How to choose

Picking the right product starts with the purpose.

The website should guide visitors toward the product that fits the actual need, not push every visitor through the same vague lead form.

  • Use a personal loan when speed matters and the borrowing size is modest
  • Use LAP when ticket size is larger and secured borrowing makes sense
  • Use working capital finance when business cash cycles are the real issue
  • Use a home loan when tenure and property-linked structuring matter most
